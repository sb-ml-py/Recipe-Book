Introduction

The Rotation is a year-long seasonal menu devided into weeks. Each week's ingredient list is suited for four people, so for a couple or single individual, the recipes can be divided or the shopping can be bi-weekly or monthly. There are four standard weeks per month with additional celebration menus for Easter, Halloween, Christmas, and New Year. Ultimately 365 days are included in the year.
